Small Project Type Warning Audit

Detailed review of the 27 no-dynamic warnings in Livebook, Credo, PhoenixLiveView, MixSBOM, OpenApiSpex, ExDoc, Spitfire, and AbsintheFederation. The audit compares against Refine and classifies whether each warning is likely a false positive.

Conclusion

16 warnings are new relative to Refine. Of those, 15 look like false positives introduced by the no-dynamic behavior, mostly integer arithmetic precision loss and Plug.Conn struct-field precision loss. 1 new warning deserves real follow-up: OpenApiSpex validate_body_schema/4 can locally pass nil to Map.get/2.

I classify “false positive” as “no real runtime fault under the source-level invariants visible in the project,” not merely “the code currently works in this compile environment.”
